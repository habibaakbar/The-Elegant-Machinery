About Us

The Elegant Machinery (TEM) established in 2017

The Elegant Machinery specializes in supplying turnkey solutions for power generation, focusing on gasoline and diesel generators ranging from 2kVA to 1000kVA. TEM provides comprehensive services including Preventive Maintenance Services (PMSs), Annual Service Level Agreements (SLAs), troubleshooting, and rental services across various generator ratings. TEM excels in supplying and servicing industrial submersible pumps and motors, offering customized solutions such as rewinding, coiling, and winding services. Whether for industrial applications or specific project requirements, TEM ensures reliable performance and tailored solutions to meet client needs.

We have successfully executed various projects, achieving an average annual turnover of PKR 100 million. Our track record includes a diverse portfolio of projects across power generation, industrial submersible pumps and motors, and related services. This consistent performance underscores our commitment to delivering quality solutions and meeting the needs of our clients effectively.

The Elegant Machinery is leading machinery supplier and service provider of Khyber Pukhtunkhwa. We provide a wide range of products and services to the national and multinational organizations, including all kind of Power & Alternate Energy, Electrical, Office, Security & IT equipment and other custom works. Quality maintenance, assurance and consumers satisfaction are what we guarantee. We always try to ensure timely delivery as per our clients requirements and needs. Our corporate operation is well equipped with motivated and skilled team players which enable the company to deliver the orders in bulk on time with perfection.

Working as a Business Trading Supplier, The Elegant Machinery has commitment, dedication and support of different business professionals and organizations of different fields to ensure best qualitative commodities by rendering best services and perfect goods required by our Consumers. Presently Elegant Machinery supplies machinery and services to Government line and staff agencies, national and International organizations, public and private Universities in KP, Pakistan.
